POLYALPHABETIC CIPHER

How does it work?

Polyalphabetic cipher is based on Caesar cipher but uses a group of keys, in a form of a word, instead of just a single number. To use the cipher

  • Enter a key word
  • Enter your message
  • Press the Cipher button

To decipher a message:

  • Enter the same key word that was used to cipher the message
  • Enter the encoded message
  • Press the Decipher button
